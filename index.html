☀ The morning of Newroz

Tonight the whole village climbs the mountain to light the great Newroz fire — the fire of spring and freedom, first lit long ago by Kawa the blacksmith.

But the four fire-keepers are scattered across the valley, each holding one piece the fire needs: the wood, the flint, the torch, and the spark.

You are Lorin. Find your four friends, help them, gather the fire — and carry it to the mountaintop before night falls.

Move: WASD / arrows · Talk & act: SPACE or the ✦ button.
